Upcoming & Past Events

Spring Events

Spring Flower Festival

Date and Time: April 10th-12th, 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM

Celebrate spring with a festival of blooming flowers! Pick your own flowers from our fields and shop a variety of locally-grown blooms and arrangements from local florists and gardeners.

Easter Egg Hunt on the Farm

Date and Time: April 20th, 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Join us for a family-friendly Easter egg hunt across the farm! Kids will search for colorful eggs hidden among the crops, with prizes for the most eggs collected. There will also be arts and crafts stations.

Summer Events

Summer Fun & Games Day

Date and Time: July 10th, 11:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Bring the whole family for a day of fun-filled games at the farm! Enjoy classic outdoor games like sack races, tug-of-war, a water balloon toss, and more. There will be friendly competitions, prizes, and plenty of opportunities to relax and enjoy the summer sun.

Past Events

Holiday Market and Bonfire

Date and Time: December 5th-6th, 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM

Shop for unique, locally-made gifts from handmade crafts to season treats and enjoy hot drinks and chat with fellow nature lovers as you reflect on the year ahead.

Fall Festival

Date and Time: October 12th, 11:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Celebrate the Fall season with a festival filled with local food vendors, live music, and fun activities for the whole family like pumpkin decorating and face painting. Shop handmade crafts and enjoy some of our favorite harvest recipes.

Apple Cider Pressing & Tasting

Date and Time: October 5th, 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM

Join us for a fun and educational experience. Learn about the apple varieties we grow, watch the cider-making process, and enjoy complimentary tastings. Apple cider donuts and other fall treats will also be available!
