About Us

Our Story

Green Farm began as a small family dream, inspired by a love for fresh fruit and the joy of being outdoors. What started as a simple backyard garden grew into a thriving U-Pick farm in East Windsor, Connecticut. We wanted to create a place where families could slow down, enjoy the beauty of nature, and experience the satisfaction of picking their own fresh, locally grown fruits.

Our Mission

Green Farm was founded with a simple mission: to reconnect people with where their food comes from. At Green Farm, we believe in sustainable farming, supporting local growers, and fostering a welcoming space for families. Our commitment to eco-friendly practices ensures that every bite you take is not only fresh but also responsibly grown.

Our Practices

We take great care in how we grow our produce. Our farm follows sustainable and eco-friendly practices, using natural methods to maintain healthy soil and vibrant crops. We rotate our fields, limit pesticides, and focus on quality over quantity—because we believe the best fruits come from a farm that's cared for with love and respect.
