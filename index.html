Pick Fresh and Create Memories

We are a family-friendly U-Pick farm! Nestled in the heart of East Windsor, Connecticut. We are dedicated to providing fresh, locally grown fruits. Our farm offers a beautiful landscape where you can enjoy the experience of picking your own produce while connecting with nature and creating lasting memories for visitors of all ages.
